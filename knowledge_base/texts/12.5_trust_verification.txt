CHAPTER 12. GROUP AND SOCIAL SURVIVAL
12.5 Trust and vetting newcomers

WHY IT MATTERS

In long crises, **people** often out-threaten weather or disease. A hostile insider can empty stores, assault members, or sell your location.

Total isolation is also fatal — you lose skills, trade, and numbers.

**Balance: trust slowly, verify specifically.**


FIRST CONTACT

Location:
— **Not** at the shelter door or inside.
— Neutral ground away from base.
— Hidden observer covering angles.

Team:
— **Minimum two** — one talks, one watches behavior and hands.
— Leader need not be the first face.

Observe:

Appearance:
— Dressed for weather or exhausted/injured?
— Open vs concealed weapons?

Behavior:
— Calm or wired?
— Allows questions or interrupts aggressively?
— Eyes constantly casing (windows, exits, your kit)?

Story:
— Specific vs vague?
— Checkable details (a real address, workplace, mutual acquaintance)?
— Why alone? (Could be fine — or a probe.)


NATURAL QUESTIONS (NOT AN INTERROGATION)

Origin:
— Where from? Family status? Route here?

Background:
— Work before crisis? Medical, electrical, mechanical skills?
— Prior group? Why leave? **Listen for evasion.**

Intent:
— What do you need **now**? Plans next?
— Willing to work for membership?

**Cross-check:** same facts, different order, days later — liars drift details.


TRIAL PERIOD

**Minimum 48–72 h** embedded labor.

During trial:
— Lives and works with you; **limited** access — meals and sleep, not armory, full cache layout, or comms crypto.
— Assign real tasks; watch follow-through.

Watch for:
— Keeps promises?
— Same behavior alone vs in group?
— Probing defenses, exits, headcount, stock levels?
— Respects others’ gear?
— Behavior toward children and sick members?

End of trial: group vote or consensus with **reasoned objections** recorded.


TRUST LEVELS

Candidate → Probationer → Full member → Trusted inner circle.

Probationer (weeks 1–2):
— No weapons issue, no full supply room keys, no strategic votes.

Full member:
— Normal access and voice per your charter.

Trusted:
— Sensitive roles, solo recon, delegation — only after long proof.


RED FLAGS AFTER ACCEPTANCE

— Hoarding food or gear secretly.
— Obsessed with weak points and schedules.
— Long unexplained absences at night.
— Whisper campaigns pitting members.
— Leaks about the group to outsiders.
— Constant inventory snooping.

Response:
— No instant public accusation.
— Private talk: leader + second.
— Increase observation.
— If confirmed threat: full group process and decision.


EXCLUSION

Triggers:
— Proven theft; internal violence; espionage for outsiders; systematic sabotage.

Process:
— Facts, not rumor.
— Accused speaks.
— Group verdict.
— If expelled: **minimum humanity pack** (food, warmth) where possible — reduces cycle of lethal internal precedent.
— Rotate security codes, routes, and watch SOPs after an insider leaves.


TRADE AND OTHER GROUPS

— Neutral meeting sites only.
— Never lead strangers to base.
— Show **trade quantity**, not warehouse depth.
— Listen more than you reveal about capabilities.

Alliances:
— Start with small swaps; scale if reliable.
— Agreements with **witnesses both sides**.
