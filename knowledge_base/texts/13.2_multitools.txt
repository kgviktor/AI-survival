CHAPTER 13. GEAR AND EQUIPMENT
13.2 Multitools and pocket tools

WHAT A MULTITOOL IS

Pliers-based or knife-based frame packing blades, drivers, saw, scissors, etc. One pocket replaces a small toolbox.

**Tradeoff:** each tool is weaker than a standalone equivalent.


MAIN TYPES

**Pliers multitool** — Leatherman / Victorinox SwissTool / Gerber pattern: pliers as core; tools in handles.

**Swiss Army Knife** — knife-centric; lighter; fewer heavy plier jobs.

**Wallet / card tool** — bottle opener, edge, small driver — backup only.

**Bit driver + bit set** — complements a multitool, not a full replacement.


KEY FEATURES

Pliers:
— Needle-nose vs standard; **integrated wire cutters** add huge utility.
— Slop in cheap pivots = junk.

Blade:
— ~6–8 cm common; wood saw very useful outdoors.

Drivers:
— Phillips #2 most used; flat medium; **exchangeable bits** expand reach.

Scissors:
— Spring-return ~5–6 cm blade minimum for real fabric.

Can opener / bottle opener — matter for long field use.

Awl — leather, cloth, starter holes.

File — small but handy.


NOTABLE LINES

**Leatherman** — industry reference; Wave+ all-rounder; Charge TTi premium; Surge heavy; Signal adds whistle/ferro/sharpener stub; Free series magnetic access.

**Victorinox** — SwissTool competes with Leatherman; compact Huntsman/Ranger SAKs for light kits.

**Gerber** — often cheaper; quality step below top Leatherman.

**Budget imports** — acceptable as **second** tool or “lose-able” spare.


SURVIVAL KIT PICKS

Primary: full-size pliers tool (Wave-class or SwissTool).

Light carry: Skeletool-class minimal or medium SAK.

Dedicated outdoors: Signal-class feature bundle (if you use the extras).

Wallet card: always-on backup, not primary.


CARRY

Belt sheath = fastest; pocket sheath; pack side pocket for travel-only.

**On body** beats “only in pack” — packs get lost.


MAINTENANCE

Wash open; toothbrush joints; light machine oil or gun oil on pivots — **wipe excess** (dust magnet).

Sharpen blade ~20–22° per side typical.

Strong warranties on major brands — worth registering.


ADD-ONS

Torx / hex / Pozi bit set if tool accepts bits.

Ceramic pocket sharpener.

Paracord wrap — spare cordage.
