CHAPTER 13. GEAR AND EQUIPMENT
13.12 Water filters: types, limits, service life

WHY FILTER

Threats: turbidity; **bacteria and cysts**; viruses (smaller); dissolved chemicals; **radioisotopes** (filters cannot fully fix dissolved ions).

**Match method to threat** — see also main water chapter (`2.x` in library).


TYPES

**1 Sediment / mechanical** — dirt only; pretreatment for everything else.

**2 Activated carbon** — taste, some organics, chlorine; **limited** on microbes and metals.

**3 Hollow-fiber membrane** — ~0.1–0.2 µm; **strong bacteria/cyst** reduction; **viruses** mostly pass; chemicals pass.

**4 Ceramic** — similar microbe class as HF; brush-cleanable; heavy.

**5 Ultrafiltration** — tighter than typical HF; some viral reduction.

**6 Reverse osmosis** — near-total dissolved solids removal; needs pressure; slow; camp/portable units exist.

**7 UV pen** — kills **clear** water biology; **pre-filter turbidity first**; batteries.

**8 Chemical** — chlorine dioxide tablets, bleach dosing, iodine (iodine not for long thyroid-safe use); crypto limits on some halogens.


COMBO FIELD KITS

**Squeeze / gravity hollow-fiber** (Sawyer, Platypus, etc.) — thousands of liters rated; **backflush** restores flow.

**Pump ceramic + carbon** — murky water tolerance with prefilters.

**Premium pump** — some units claim **virus** protection with complex cartridges — verify test data.

**Berkey-style gravity** — base camp; carbon elements; maintain per maker.


LIMITS

— **Biology filters do not remove dissolved chemical plume** or fuel — carbon helps some VOCs, not all; **avoid the source**.

— **Radiation:** particulates can be filtered **if** bound to sediment; dissolved radionuclides need different methods — seek clean source.


SERVICE

— Slow flow → **backflush** HF (syringe or clean water reverse).

— **Never freeze** wet hollow fibers — destroys pores.

— Long storage: dry per instructions or mild chlorine rinse per maker to prevent biofilm.

— Replace carbon when taste changes or rated liters reached.


RECOMMENDED STACK

**Minimum:** hollow-fiber squeeze + **chlorine dioxide** or boil backup.

**Better:** HF + UV on clear output for viral worry regions.

**Shelter:** multi-stage bucket (gravel/sand/char) **+** boil or chemical — char is not sterile by itself.
