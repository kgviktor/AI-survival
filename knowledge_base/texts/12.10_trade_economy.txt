CHAPTER 12. GROUP AND SOCIAL SURVIVAL
12.10 Trade, barter, and primitive economy

WHY TRADE RETURNS FAST

Even in collapse, **gaps differ** between groups — meds here, tools there. Barter begins in days. Markets appeared in camps, sieges, and occupations worldwide — deep human pattern.


WHAT BECOMES “MONEY”

No cash — value = **urgent need × scarcity**.

**Top tier** (hard to get, life-critical):
— Antibiotics, insulin, strong analgesics.
— Ammo / weapons (legal and ethical rules still apply in many regions).
— Fuels.
— **Seeds.**
— **Salt** — preservation, physiology, taste.

**Durable trade goods:**
— Spirits — stable, stress relief, antiseptic uses.
— Tobacco — addictive demand.
— Coffee/tea — morale.
— Precious metals — **later**; week one people want calories.
— Quality tools, fabric, thread.

**Consumables:**
— Food, clean water, firewood winter, batteries, candles, lighters.

**Services:**
— Medic, smith, electrician — traded for food and security.


BARGAINING PRINCIPLES

1. Know your surplus and shortage **before** the meet.
2. **Needy face = bad price.** Calm walk-away power.
3. Show **trade lot**, not warehouse depth.
4. Short-term gouging vs long-term **fair dealer** reputation — usually favor repeat partners.
5. Big swaps: **witnesses both sides**; write what exchanged.


LOCAL MARKET (WHEN STABLE)

— Neutral ground; regular schedule attracts traffic.
— **Truce zone** norms — theft or violence at market = collective ban.
— Weapons: clear local rule — concealed, peace-bonded, or banned.
— Standard measures: “can of flour,” “bundle of sticks,” agreed cup — cuts arguments.

Mediator:
— Trusted referee; small fee — evolves into steward role.


DEBT AND CREDIT

High default risk when people die or flee.

— Strangers: **cash on the barrel** — swap goods now.
— Known partners: small IOU with date/event trigger.
— Large debt: **collateral** held.

Promissory notes:
— Written, signed, witnessed: “A owes B 2 kg salt by [date].”


EMERGING INSTRUMENTS

— IOUs that circulate (proto-bills).
— **Unit of account** — “one 9 mm round,” “one matchbox,” journal credits — need not be physical coin.
— Communal strongbox — proto-bank if trust holds.

Specialization:
— Stable food production frees smiths, medics, guards — classic division of labor lifts total output.


DEALING WITH ARMED AUTHORITY

— Avoid **single** supplier monopolies — they price-gouge.
— Scout what they want before you show up.
— Never flash total stock.
— First offer favors them — negotiate.

“Tax” / protection rake:
— Judge real safety vs extortion.
— Alliances with other groups; alternate routes; documented abuse if law returns.
