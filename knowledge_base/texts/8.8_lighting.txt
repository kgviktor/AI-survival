CHAPTER 8. ENERGY AND ELECTRONICS
8.8 Lighting: flashlights, DIY lamps, candles

WHY LIGHT MATTERS

Darkness limits work, increases injury risk, hurts morale, blocks reading/study.

Goals:
— Task lighting.
— Minimal night safety glow.
— Signal light when needed.
— Light discipline — not visible outside when hiding.


LED FLASHLIGHTS / HEADLAMPS

LEDs dominate off-grid: ~5–10× incandescent efficiency; long life; rugged.

Headlamp: hands-free — ~100–200 lm general work; red mode preserves night vision.
Carry 2–3 + spare cells.

Handheld/spot: outdoor search — 500–2000+ lm possible; 18650/26650 cells.

Camp lanterns: diffuse room/tent light.

12 V LED strips/lamps: direct on battery — ~5 W/m strips common; G4/MR16 12 V bulbs.

Cells:
— NiMH AA/AAA rechargeable; 18650 for high-power lights.

Alkaline stock:
— AA/AAA buffer stock if no recharge — cool dry storage ~years.


CANDLES

Ancient reliable light — no grid.

~20 mm pillar ~6–10 h burn; ~1 candela — lights ~1–2 m.

**Fire risk** — never unattended; stable base; clear combustibles; ventilation — consumes O₂, adds CO₂/soot.

Boost candle output:
— Foil/mirror reflector behind.
— Can lantern with slots — directional glow.

Stock: many small candles or large pillar ~100+ h.


DIY OIL LAMP

Wick draws oil to flame.

Vessel: jar or tin.
Fuel: vegetable oil, animal fat, mineral oil, liquid paraffin — not gasoline.
Wick: braided cotton; 3–5 mm above oil after soak.

Trim charred wick for clean flame.
~15–20 ml/h small lamp — ~50–70 h per liter oil rough.


RECAST CANDLES FROM stubs

Double-boil wax; center wick in mold; pour; cool; demold.
Stearin hardens wax; scents optional.


KEROSENE / PARAFFIN LAMP

Brighter than candle (~10–20 cd class).
Maintain globe; trim wick square; adjust for smoke-free flame.
Fuel: lamp oil, liquid paraffin — not volatile gasoline.


SHELTER LIGHT DISCIPLINE

Blackout curtains before lights.
Minimum needed — avoid ceiling bounce out windows.
Red filter near openings — less visible outside, preserves night vision.

Zones:
— Bright only at work sites.
— Dark sleep zone.
— Dim corridor path lights.

Backup per room: flashlight or candle + fire starter.
UPS night light: charges on grid, works on outage if unit healthy.
