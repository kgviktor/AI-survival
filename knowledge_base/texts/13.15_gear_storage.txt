CHAPTER 13. GEAR AND EQUIPMENT
13.15 Storing and maintaining gear

READINESS PRINCIPLE

Gear fails you if you **cannot find it**, it is **dead battery / dull / empty**, or you **never practiced** with it.

**Goal:** grab-and-go functional state 24/7.


STORAGE LAYERS

**On body:** knife, multitool, small light, fire, phone if carried.

**Immediate pouch:** EDC med; water bottle; spare cells; radio.

**Go-bag by exit:** full evacuation kit — **30-second** grab.

**Shelter cache:** long food, fuel, bulk med, duplicates.


LABEL AND LOG

Container exterior: **contents list** + last check date + expiry notes for food/drugs/cells.

Master spreadsheet: item / qty / location / condition / checked.

**Color code** optional: red med, blue comms/nav, green food/water, orange tools, yellow docs/cash.


MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE

**Monthly:** top lights/radio/powerbank; visual gear pass; bug-out bag present?

**Quarterly:** clothing zippers, seam tape, DWR; **knife/axe edge**; boot soles; **radio check**; generator exercise **outdoors** with load; med expiry sweep; food rotation.

**Annual:** full inventory audit; replace expired; wash sleep systems; oil steel tools; **document copies** refresh; cash in emergency stash checked for damage; **wear the pack 2–3 km** — adjust fit and contents.


ITEM-SPECIFIC

**Knives:** sheathed; oiled carbon.

**Firearms (where legal):** secured locked storage; rules vary — follow law; clean after use; **ammo separate** per safe practice.

**Electronics:** remove **alkaline** cells for long storage (leak damage); Li-ion ~50% SOC; silica gel in box; avoid car summer heat for batteries.

**Documents:** laminated or waterproof pouch; copies off-site.

**Food:** cool, dark, dry; **FIFO**; oxygen absorbers for grains optional.

**Moisture:** pack liner + dry bags inside; desiccant in tubs; inspect for mold.


EVACUATION WEIGHT

**Three-zone rule:** body = survive 24–72 h if pack gone; pack = main kit; vehicle = bulky extras.

**Target:** ≤~15–20 kg pack for fast foot movement if you must.

**Pre-flight checklist:** water ≥2 L; food ≥3 days; fire triple; light + cells; shelter; med L2; compass + map; comms + charge; knife + tool; docs; cash.
